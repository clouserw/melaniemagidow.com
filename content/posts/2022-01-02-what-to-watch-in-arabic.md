In this chilly winter season, here’s a list of Arabic shows and movies with something for everyone to enjoy…

- <a href="/images/04d39-justice.png">![](/images/04d39-justice.png)</a><figcaption>Justice</figcaption>
- <a href="/images/2d49e-secret.of_.the_.nile_.png">![](/images/Secret.of_.the_.Nile_-1024x669.png)</a><figcaption>Secret of the Nile</figcaption>
- <a href="/images/e439e-wadjda.png">![](/images/e439e-wadjda.png)</a><figcaption>Wadjda</figcaption>

- **Justice** | Netflix | UAE | Crime / Courtroom TV Show | about a young woman lawyer (I liked it!)

- **Secret of the Nile** | Egypt | Netflix | Mystery, Drama, Romance | a beautiful reinvention of the Spanish telenovela Grand Hotel (one of my favorites!)

- **Wadjda** | Saudi Arabia | Netflix | Movie, Social Issue Drama, Heartfelt | about a girl growing up

- <a href="/images/9bd9e-barakah.meets_.barakah.png">![](/images/9bd9e-barakah.meets_.barakah.png)</a>
- <a href="/images/98a7f-alis.wedding.png">![](/images/98a7f-alis.wedding.png)</a>
- <a href="/images/a6720-peasants.rebellion.png">![](/images/a6720-peasants.rebellion.png)</a>

<figcaption>Barakah Meets Barakah, Ali’s Wedding, Peasants’ Rebellion</figcaption>

- **Barakah Meets Barakah** | Saudi Arabia | Netflix | Romantic Comedy Movie, Quirky | (I liked it!)

- **Ali’s Wedding** | Australia (Arab community in Australia, speaking Shami/Levantine Arabic) | Netflix | Romantic Comedy Movie, Irreverent, Emotional | Like *My Big Fat Greek Wedding*

- **Peasants’ Rebellion** | Lebanon | Netflix | Historical Drama, Social Issues, TV series

- <a href="/images/a2ba5-west-beirut.png">![](/images/a2ba5-west-beirut.png)</a>
- <a href="/images/8f07a-paranormal.png">![](/images/8f07a-paranormal.png)</a>
- <a href="/images/aa151-jinn.png">![](/images/aa151-jinn.png)</a>

<figcaption>West Beirut, Paranormal, Jinn</figcaption>

- **Paranormal** | Egypt | Netflix | Chilling, Scary, Horror, TV series

- **Jinn** | Jordan | Netflix | Ominous, Suspenseful, Fantasy, TV series

- **West Beirut** | Lebanon | Netflix | Movie, Irreverent | Two teenage boys growing up during the Lebanese civil war

- <a href="/images/fa295-black.money_.love_.png">![](/images/fa295-black.money_.love_.png)</a><figcaption>Black Money Love</figcaption>
- <a href="/images/36e39-from.japan_.to_.egypt_.png">![](/images/From.Japan_.To_.Egypt_-1024x571.png)</a><figcaption>From Japan to Egypt</figcaption>
- <a href="/images/4b5e3-excuse.my_.french.png">![](/images/4b5e3-excuse.my_.french.png)</a><figcaption>Excuse My French</figcaption>

- **Black Money Love** | Turkey (available dubbed in Shami/Levantine Arabic) | Netflix | Crime TV Show, Drama, Emotional, Soapy

- **From Japan to Egypt** | Egypt | Prime Video | Quirky Comedy | About an Egyptian man, separated from his Japanese wife, who wants to raise his kids in Egypt

- **لا مؤاخذة “No Offense”** (Translated as “Excuse My French”) | Egypt | YT: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y58uVGh6xRs&t=1s](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y58uVGh6xRs&t=1s)

A Christian kid suddenly is forced to go to a public school after his father dies and because of a misunderstanding everyone thinks that he’s a Muslim.

*Other possible sources: Kanopy (especially for documentaries), Hoopla (especially for language-learning resources) – both available through public libraries*
