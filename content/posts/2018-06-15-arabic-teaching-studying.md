#### IV. Gulf ~ خليجي

## Videos & Music

- Check out [Playaling](https://playaling.com/) for tons of videos in various dialects/language varieties, with downloadable. You can search by language level, activate captions, and adjust other settings.

- The YouTube channel [MohCoolMan](https://www.youtube.com/user/MohCoolMan) offers a wide range of songs (from Disney songs, to pop, to classics), with lyrics added in Arabic and in English translation. It’s not perfect, but it’s amazing as a free resource.

## Recommended for teachers looking for course materials:

- [Lisan Arabi](http://www.arabinglish.com/) – has a Facebook page and an Instagram account. According to the Facebook page, Lisan Arabi “is an attempt to introduce the Arabic Language and to render some relevant services to people who are interested in Arabic language and culture.” It provides vocabulary quizzes and useful visual tools for learning new vocabulary
