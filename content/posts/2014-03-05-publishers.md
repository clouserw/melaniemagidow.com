I’m starting a list of publishers here for literature of the Middle East and North Africa. Tell me if you know of more that should be added…[And here is another helpful list](https://arablit.org/2016/04/30/for-indie-bookstore-day-26-great-arab-bookshops-recommended-by-writers-translators-and-scholars/).

## Egypt

- [CairoBookStop](http://cairobookstop.wordpress.com/) / محطة كتب القاهرة is a great tool for locating books in Cairo, designed by Nancy Linthicum and Michele Henjum

- Dar al-Hilal / دار الهلال- affordable paperback literature, also a magazine

- [Dar al-Shorouk](http://www.shorok.com/) / دار الشروق – fiction and non-fiction, in Arabic

- [L’Orientaliste](http://www.orientalecairo.com/) bookstore – See review [here](http://cairobooklore.blogspot.com/2014/12/lorientaliste-or-for-love-of-books.html).

- [Diwan](https://www.diwanegypt.com/default.aspx) / ديوان bookstore

- [American University in Cairo Press](http://www.aucpress.com/) – fiction and non-fiction, in English

- For international orders, I can recommend [Neelwafurat](http://neelwafurat.com/)

## England

- [Saqi Books](http://www.saqibooks.co.uk/) – academic and general books – As of Dec. 2022, the London store is closed, although publishing continues.

## Lebanon

- [Dar Saqi](http://www.daralsaqi.com/) / دار الساقي – fiction and non-fiction, in Arabic

## Morocco

- [Editions Le Fennec](http://www.lefennec.com/) / دار الفنك – especially for Francophone literature

- [Librairie Samir](http://www.samirediteur.com/new/historique.asp) / سمير – especially for children’s literature, Arabic and French

## United Arab Emirates

- [Kalimat](http://www.kalimat.ae/) / كلمات – children’s and young adult literature in Arabic

## United States

- [Interlink Books](http://www.interlinkbooks.com/) – a publishing group including many regions, among them MENA

- [University of Texas Press](http://utpress.utexas.edu/) – academic books and literary translations, in English
